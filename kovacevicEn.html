Kovačević Winery - something special!

In order to improve wine culture and wine routes, Miroslav Kovačević took over the old Irish cellar "Cellar 1930" in 2013.

This grandiose building of a unique ambient whole is very important for the town of Irig, both because of its history and because of the preservation and cultivation of wine culture in this region.


Our vision is a constant search for the best wines And it never stops. This search has led us to turn to the production of wines of specific styles according to the oldest wine techniques, such as long maceration of grapes and the production of wine in amphorae, whose tradition dates back several thousand years. A step further is the preservation of tradition and the return to nature, so we have dedicated ourselves to organic production. As there are no stops in viticulture and winemaking, we are slowly stepping into the future, exploring biodynamic viticulture.
