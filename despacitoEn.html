Restaurant Despacito-ideal for meetings!

The Despacito restaurant exudes a unique interior enriched with modern elements. It exudes elegance, but at the same time a very pleasant atmosphere. If you want to impress a business associate or client, the Despacito restaurant is ideal for a business meeting, and its ambience will help make any deal.

It is a great place for family gatherings, as well as for hanging out with friends, especially in the warm months when you can enjoy a large garden full of greenery. Restaurant Despacito in New Belgrade is a perfect choice for celebrating important life events such as birthdays and baptisms, as well as major celebrations, since it has a capacity of 120 seats.


The menu of the restaurant is very diverse, with a wide range of various international specialties. Here you can try first-class sea fish, the highest quality meat prepared with the special skills of excellent chefs, as well as several types of pasta that is made in the restaurant itself. Since the Despacito restaurant strives to be a place where everyone will feel comfortable, the menu is also rich in vegetarian dishes. In accordance with the menu, the wine list of the Despacito restaurant is very extensive and contains many wines from the region and abroad.
