The best steaks in Belgrade - Babaroga!

I live abroad, I came to Belgrade for a couple of days and I visited Babaroga steakhouse - they left me completely delighted!

At first glance, it can be seen that every detail was taken into account, while the ambience of this restaurant is really impressive. Decorated in a modern-traditional style, this restaurant combines the best of the two worlds. The whole wall covered with greenery brings freshness and serenity to the whole space, so you have the impression that you are sitting in a blossoming garden.


Ethno elements of the interior give a special charm to the whole space, which exudes a homely and pleasant atmosphere that makes this restaurant an ideal choice for family lunch, business meeting, casual socializing with friends and romantic dinner. Serbian cuisine is presented here in a slightly different spirit the flavors are not lost - they just come to the fore.
